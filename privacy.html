Privacy Policy


AquaFold Inc. Privacy Policy

Date of last revision: April 2, 2010

This policy applies to all of AquaFold Inc. This policy does not apply to companies that AquaFold does not control or individuals that are not employed or managed by AquaFold.

NOTICE

When you register with AquaFold, use AquaFold products or services, AquaFold collects your personal information. Such information includes:

Personally identifiable information - information that can be used to uniquely identify, contact, or locate a single person or can be used with other sources to uniquely identify a single individual. It includes but is not limited to name, home or business address, e-mail address and, phone number.

Demographic information - characteristics of a human population that cannot identify a single person but help to determine a subgroup of the population a person belongs to. Demographic information includes but is not limited to age, gender, ZIP code, education, profession, experience and interests.

If you choose to subscribe for a paid service or make a purchase, AquaFold will collect payment specific information such as credit card or bank account number and billing address.

AquaFold through its applications automatically receives and records some information from your computer and web browser. Such information includes but is not limited to computer IP address, cookie information, and software and hardware attributes of your system.

AquaFold processes collected personally identifiable information solely for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Such purposes include:

(a) Identification of users of AquaFold products and services;
(b) Fulfillment of requested products or services;
(c) Maintenance of functioning of AquaFold products and services;
(d) Providing user support for AquaFold products and services;
(e) Protecting rights and property of AquaFold and its users;
(f) Communication with you regarding business of AquaFold or its partners;
(g) Production of aggregated non-personal information - information that is recorded and collected into groups so that it no longer references a particular person;
(h) Conducting audit and analysis for improvement of AquaFold products and services and development of new products and services;
(i) Promotion of newly developed AquaFold products and services and improvements for existing products and services;
(j) Providing you with customized content, products or services.

CHOICE

At the time of registration you can decline submitting personal information to AquaFold. However AquaFold may not be able to provide some or all of its products or services to you.

You can choose to prevent using your personal information for any purpose other than described in this Privacy Policy. For any offer that deals with use of your personal information other than for the purposes defined in this Privacy Policy, AquaFold will provide you a way to opt out of the other use.

AquaFold will not request or use sensitive information without your explicit consent.

You can choose to disable cookies in your browser. In such case AquaFold will not be able to collect personal information received by its applications from cookies. However some of the functionality of AquaFold services and products may be disabled.

ONWARD TRANSFER

Transfers of your personal information from AquaFold to external individuals or non-affiliated business entities are limited to the following circumstances:

(a) AquaFold has an explicit consent from you;
(b) AquaFold is obliged to do so by applicable law;
(c) AquaFold has to respond to court orders, subpoenas, or legal process, or to exercise its legal rights and defend against legal claims;
(d) AquaFold in good faith believes that disclosure of personal information is necessary to prevent or take actions against illegal activities, harm to the rights or property of AquaFold and its users as it is permitted by law;

If AquaFold becomes involved in a process of merger, acquisition or sale of its assets, you will be notified before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

AquaFold reserves rights to share aggregated non-personal information with external business entities or individuals. This information cannot be used to identify you.

SECURITY

AquaFold adopts best industry practices based on technology and corporate policies to protect personal data against unauthorized disclosure, alteration or deletion. Security measures include:

(a) AquaFold always offers password protected access to the user profile page.
(b) AquaFold stores personal information on computer systems with limited access, which are located in access controlled facilities.
(c) AquaFold restricts access to personal information to only its employees and contractors who need this information in order to operate, support or develop products and services of AquaFold. All employees and contractors are bound to confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements with AquaFold prior to receiving access to personal information.

DATA INTEGRITY

AquaFold collects and stores personal information relevant to the purposes for which the information is used by its products and services.

AquaFold takes technical measures to prevent collected private information from corruption and loss due to the equipment malfunctioning and failure. Such measures include systematic back-ups of data collection, data restoration procedures, implementation of intentionally redundant computer and data storage systems.

Under terms of the User Agreement AquaFold encourages users to cooperate towards keeping personal data accurate and current by providing accurate information, correcting or reporting inaccuracies whenever necessary.
